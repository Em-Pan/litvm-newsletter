## Featured Story

### LiteForge testnet is live

LitVM's LiteForge testnet went live on April 15, bringing smart contracts, DeFi, and Web3 applications to the Litecoin ecosystem.

The launch marks a major step toward Hard Money Web3: a programmable application layer built around Litecoin's long-standing strengths: uptime, liquidity, proof-of-work security, and cultural trust.

With LitVM and zkLTC, builders can now begin testing the applications Litecoin has been missing: DEXs, lending markets, prediction markets, RWA platforms, AI-native applications, payment rails, and more.

This is not just another testnet launch. It is the first serious opening for Litecoin to compete as a modern Web3 settlement and application ecosystem.

## Highlights

### 1. The Hard Money Web3 Manifesto set the tone

Before LiteForge went live, we published the Hard Money Web3 Manifesto: a line in the sand for what LitVM stands for and what Web3 should become.

The manifesto explains the deeper reason behind LitVM: Web3 needs stronger monetary foundations, not just faster chains and louder narratives. Litecoin already has the longevity, proof-of-work roots, and hard-money culture. LitVM adds the missing programmable layer.

CTA: Read the manifesto
URL: https://x.com/LitecoinVM/status/2043735711686504557

### 2. Goldsky joined as LitVM's subgraph indexing partner

Goldsky, the data backbone behind Polymarket, Privy, Monad, and other major Web3 teams, was announced as LitVM's subgraph indexing partner.

This gives LitVM builders access to the kind of real-time data infrastructure needed for serious applications: analytics, dashboards, indexing, event monitoring, and production-grade app experiences.

For developers, this means building on LitVM will feel less like starting from scratch and more like plugging into an ecosystem that is ready for real usage.

CTA: View Goldsky announcement
URL: https://x.com/LitecoinVM/status/2041933639626408242

### 3. LitVM was selected for CoinMarketCap's CMC Labs accelerator

LitVM was selected to join CoinMarketCap's CMC Labs accelerator, a major visibility milestone for both LitVM and the Litecoin ecosystem.

CoinMarketCap reaches millions of crypto users, investors, and builders worldwide. Joining CMC Labs gives LitVM a stronger path to introduce Hard Money Web3 to a much larger audience as the testnet grows.

The message is simple: Litecoin's Web3 expansion is no longer a niche conversation. It is entering the broader market.

### 4. The Litecoin Vanguard Collective launched

LitVM launched the Litecoin Vanguard Collective alongside the Litecoin Foundation and Luxxfolio.

The collective is a new hub for the broader Litecoin ecosystem: community members, miners, builders, merchants, enterprises, developers, and long-term supporters who want to help Litecoin move into its next era.

LitVM is infrastructure, but ecosystems are built by people. The Vanguard Collective gives that community a shared place to organize, collaborate, and push Litecoin forward.

### 5. Aztec Amaya introduced new LitVM research

LitVM Co-Founder Aztec Amaya showcased the new research paper, `A Hard Money Foundation for a Unified Web3`.

The paper explores the current state of blockchain technology, the challenges facing Web3, and the role blockchain abstraction can play in creating a more unified application layer.

For LitVM, this research anchors the bigger vision: making Litecoin a foundation for modern decentralized applications without forcing users and builders through fragmented, chain-by-chain complexity.

Hard Money Web3 is becoming a live conversation on Litecoin's biggest stages.

### 7. DIA joined as LitVM's official oracle partner

DIA joined as LitVM's official oracle infrastructure provider.

Reliable price data is critical for DeFi. Lending markets, DEXs, RWA platforms, prediction markets, and other onchain applications all need verifiable, high-quality data to function safely.

With DIA, LitVM builders get access to enterprise-grade oracle infrastructure sourcing from 100+ CEXs and DEXs, covering 20,000+ assets across the broader crypto economy.
